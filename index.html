Get Your Own Website For Free!

What you need to do:

  1. Create a word document that looks like the website you wanted.

  2. Add a title on the first line of your document that will appear on the tabs bar and a title on the second line of our document that will appear as part of the URL ("https://alanmencan.github.io/The title/").

  3. Send/share your document to "alanmencan@gmail.com".

  4. Wait until you got a email with the URL for your new website.

  5. Copy and paste the URL and you will see your new website!


Please note:

  1. The URL will always look like the following, with only a little part of it that you can control. Like this: "https://alanmencan.github.io/This is the part of the URL that you can change/".

  2. If something went wrong, you can send a new email with the URL to complain. I will try to fix it as soon as possible.
